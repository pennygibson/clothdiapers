Take The Test

Find Out Now if You Should Cloth Diaper?

Answer "Yes" or "No" to the following list of questions. The more yeses you have the more likely it is that you SHOULD use cloth diapers. If you answer no to more than three of the questions than cloth diapering probably isn't for you.

  • I can afford the initial upfront cost of cloth diapers ($300-$700)
  • I have easy access to a washing machine and dryer
  • My partner is supportive/ on board with using cloth diapers
  • I consider myself eco-conscious and work to lessen my carbon footprint
  • I enjoy learning new skills and taking on a challenge
  • I tend to be someone who looks at 'the big picture' and am more future oriented
  • It bothers me that disposable diapers take over 500 years to decompose
  • It concerns me that disposable diapers use known cancer causing chemicals
  • I see the pros of using cloth diapers as outweighing the cons
  • My initial reaction to the idea of using cloth was positive, I thought it was a great idea!
  • I've put some thought into cloth diapering and have taken the time to do some research
  • I believe that I am capable of using cloth diapers and that I will find it easy once I know the basics and how tos
  • I really want to use cloth diapers
